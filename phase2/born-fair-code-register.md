# Born-fair-code / non-canonical LP — observational register

Created: 2026-06-07
Status: **watch-list — NOT a hypothesis.** No validation criterion. Pure observation.

## Qué es este registro

Cases born under source-available / fair-code licensing that later **harden** licensing further, **without** ever having had an OSI-open core. Per the sealed rule in `../HYPOTHESIS_LP_DETONANTE_VS_DEFENSOR.md` § Definición de "LP-present" (2026-06-07), these do **NOT** count toward the canonical LP-present N=10 of the detonator-vs-defender hypothesis, and cannot satisfy its discriminant contraejemplos.

They are logged here so the observation is not lost. A dedicated hypothesis with its own ex-ante criterion is formulated **only if/when** this register reaches a size that warrants it. With N=1 today, formulating one would be a pseudo-hypothesis — deliberately not done.

## Por qué se separa (no inflar el denominador)

La hipótesis principal teoriza el rol direccional de un pivot **desde abierto** hacia restrictivo. Un caso born-fair-code nunca hizo ese movimiento: instancia un fenómeno distinto (source-available desde origen → source-available más restrictivo). Mezclarlo en el mismo denominador confundiría dos poblaciones.
